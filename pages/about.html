This is a very personal project. It's regarding my closet and my style and the things I want, need and have. I have always had an interest in clothing and textiles, more so than an interest in fashion and trends. This website serves two functions: firstly, to create a visual structure of what I need to buy, make and mend, and secondly to practice on creating websites.

I have a lot of thoughts related to style and clothing and I need somewhere to sort it out, so I might add some type of blog typ section here. A few years back I started a company where I focused on creating sustainable fashion in small quantities, mostly unique objects with hand embroidery, painted patterns and plant dyed fabrics. Then I ran headfirst in to wall, as we say in Sweden. I worked, studied full time and ran a company on the side. One day I woke up and was unable to get out of bed. Months of pushing through the tiredness, the anxiety and ignoring all types of signs that I was doing well had caught up with me. It took me years to get back to a place where I feel good about myself and my situation. During these years I kind of lost a lot of myself and my creativity.

So this project is a way of getting back in to it. To structure my thoughts and my feelings and all of the things that are related to clothing and style. And there is a lot, from expressing yourself to environmental issues to working conditions in the fashion industry.
